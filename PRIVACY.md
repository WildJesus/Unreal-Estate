# Privacy Policy — Unreal Estate

**Last updated: 2026-04-14**

Unreal Estate does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

- No personal information is collected.
- No browsing history or activity is recorded.
- No data is sent to any external server. Ever.
- All economic data (mortgage rates, price indices, wages) is static and bundled inside the extension package. No network requests are made.

The only data stored locally on your device is your UI preferences (language choice and auto-open toggle), saved via `chrome.storage` and never leaving your browser.
